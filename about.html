Dinner Wizard is an application that provides suggestions on what dish or dishes to make, given a list of ingredients users already have.

Inspired by The New York Times Cooking 'No-Recipe Wednesdays', Dinner Wizard is meant to help encourage improvisation and experimentation with ingredients while cooking by avoiding giving measurements and phrasing instructions more loosely than what might be expected in a cookbook.

All suggested ingredient ratios are given by volume, uncooked. Similarly, all ingredients listed are assumed to be unprepared, unless otherwise noted.

    An Explanation of Search Filters:
  • Spicy: only show combinations that are spicy
  • Vegetarian: only show vegetarian combinations
  • Under 30(minutes) for preparation
  • Chinese: Flavors derivative of Chinese food
  • Italian: Flavors derivative of Italian food
  • Mediterranean: Flavors derivative of those found around the Mediterranean Sea
  • Southeast Asian: Flavors derivative of some of those found in Southeast Asia
  • Western: This kind of food can probably be found in North America and may not fit any of the filters listed previously
